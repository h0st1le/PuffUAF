Particle Distributions

This controls the number distribution of particles as a function of height. linear initializes particles evenly from the bottom to the top of the plume. poisson is a poisson distribution with most particles near the top of the plume. The spread of the 'bulge' is determined by the parameter plumeZwidth (in km). exponential initializes most particles at the plume top and exponentially decreases downward until the plume bottom. The parameter plumeZwidth determines how steeply the decrease occurs.
